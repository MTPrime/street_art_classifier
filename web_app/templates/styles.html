Realistic Style

This is probably the most self explanatory style. Pieces done in the realistic style try to capture scenes close to real-life. The colors are often less vibrant than the other styles, but not always.

Wildstyle

This tagging style is made up of sharp geometric and interlocking shapes. It can be difficult to decipher words written in this style, but it is very striking in appearance.

Bubble

This is another tagging style like Wildstyle, but here the letters are rounded and curved, creating a bubble like apeparance. It's often done in multiple colors to give a 3D affect to the letters.

Cartoon

This is a broad style with topics ranging from super heros to surreal scenes to unique creations. The colors are vibrant and span the color spectrum.

Stencil

This artwork is named after the stencils that it uses. Artist create models out of cardboard or other materials and either use spray paint or a rolling brush to apply the paint. This is the style Banksy uses.
