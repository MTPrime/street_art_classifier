Realistic Style

This is probably the most self explanatory of the different styles. Pieces done in the realistic style try to capture scenes close to real-life. The colors are often less vibrant than the other styles, but not always.

Wildstyle

This tagging style is made up of sharp geometric, interlocking shapes. It can be difficult to decipher words written in this style, but it is very striking in appearance.

Bubble

This is another tagging style like Wildstyle, but here the letters are rounded and curved, creating a bubble like apeparance. Often it is done in multiple colors to give a 3d affect to the letters.

Stencil

This artwork is named after the stencils that it uses. Artist create mdoels out of cardboard or other materials and either spraypaint or a rolling brush. Notable artist Banksy uses this style.
