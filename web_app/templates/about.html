About my project


Background

One of my hobbies when I travel is to seek out and photograph local street art. I wanted to see if I could make a model that would be able to take a picture of street art and classify its art style as well as recommend other similar pictures.


Data

The data all came from fatcap.com, a graffiti website that has been around since 1998 and collected over 22,000 images of street art classified into 18 styles. I ended up focusing on a smaller subset of 5 styles and only images that were painted onto a wall. (Boby paint and street cars would have added an additional challenge for the CNN.)
